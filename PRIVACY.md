## Summary

TAKE 47 is designed to work without collecting your personal information. Your game progress stays on your device. We don't ask for your name, email, location, or contacts, and the game is fully playable offline.

## What the app stores

Game progress (current scene, furthest scene, take counts, prints, achievements, unlocked characters) and settings (sound, reduce motion) are stored **only on your device**. Deleting the app deletes this data. We have no servers and no account system; we cannot see your progress.

## What the app does NOT do

No accounts or sign-in. No collection of name, email, phone, location, contacts, photos, or health data. No analytics SDKs. No selling of data — we have none to sell.

## Sharing features

The share button creates an image on your device and hands it to iOS's standard share sheet. Where you send it is between you and the app you choose; nothing is transmitted to us.

## Apple

Apple provides us aggregate, anonymized statistics about the app (download counts, crash reports) through App Store Connect, governed by Apple's own privacy terms and your device settings. This tells us nothing about you individually.
